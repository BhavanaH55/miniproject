BEST OF TRIPURA
Day 1

ARRIVAL IN AGARTALA

Your amazing Tripura vacation with Tour My India will start with your arrival at onward destination, which would be the Agartala Airport.At the airport, our representative will be waiting to escort you to a pre-booked hotel.After getting freshened up, be ready to go on a sightseeing tour of Agartala.On the tour, you will visit popular attractions like Laxm Mandir, Ujjayanta Palace and Jagannath Temple.You will be also taken for a shopping tour in local markets where you’ll get to see the bamboo handicrafts.Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2

AGARTALA TO UDAIPUR TO AGARTALA

Your first morning on this amazing tour will start with a scrumptious breakfast.Post breakfast, proceed towards Udaipur. On the way, enjoy an amazing wildlife tour to Sipahijala Wildlife Sanctuary.Upon arrival at Udaipur, visit popular tourist places like Matabari Temple, Kali Temple and Mela Ghar where you will visite Neer Mahal.After exploring all these places, head back to Agartala.Overnight stay and dinner will be provided at the same hotel in Agartala.

Day 3

AGARTALA SIGHTSEEING

In the morning, you will be served with a scrumptious breakfast.Followed by this, proceed a drive towards Dumboor Lake. Here you’ll have the opportunity to relish some astonishing views of the hills from the group of 48 islands.While spending a day here, you will also indulge in bird watching and boating activities.For dinner and overnight stay, you will be accommodated back to the hotel.

Day 4

AGARTALA TO UNAKOTI

Like all days, start your morning with a delicious breakfast.Post breakfast, begin a drive to Unakoti. This would be a 7-8 hours drive.Upon arrival, be ready to explore a hillock which is dotted with the ancient rock sculptures.Thereafter, go on a sightseeing tour of the region.Followed by this, drive towards Kamlasagar to have dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5

SILCHAR TO DEPARTURE

Enjoy a nutritious breakfast in the morning.After breakfast, be ready with your luggage to end to this amazing expedition.You will be taken to Silchar Airport to board the flight back to the onwards destination or your home.
