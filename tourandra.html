BEAUTY OF ANDRA PRADESH
Day 1

ARRIVE DELHI

Arrive Delhi on arrival Garlanding with a traditional Indian welcome and transferred to the Hotel. Afternoon city tour of Old and New Delhi offers a stunning contrast between the ancient and modern..

Day 2

DELHI - HYDERABAD

After a morning at leisure, late afternoon transfer to airport time to board flight for Hyderabad, now the capital of Andhra Pradesh, on arrival transfer to the hotel. O/N at Hotel.

Day 3

GOLCONDA FORT EXCURSION - HYDERABAD SIGHTSEEING

Morning visit fort of Golconda is the former capital of the Qutub Shahi dynasty. Afternoon visit of the city. O/N at hotel.

Day 4

THE BACKWATERS CRUISE

Morning enjoy a boat ride through the series of canals and lagoons snaking their way through several tiny villages and tropical vegetation. O/N at Hotel.

Day 5

COCHIN - KUMARAKOM

Depart this morning for Kumarakom on arrival check in at Hotel. Afternoon is at leisure O/N at Hotel.

Day 6

CHENNAI SIGHTSEEING - DEPART CHENNAI

Morning tour of Chennai. Afternoon free. Late evening transfer to the Intl Airport time to board flight for onward destination.
