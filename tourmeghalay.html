BEST OF MEGHALAYA
Day 1

ARRIVE GUWAHATI

Our representative will meet and greet you at Guwahati airport or railway station. Transfer to the hotel. Later visit Kamakhya Devi temple and after that visit Kalakshetra. Evening you can enjoy river cruise on mighty Brambhaputra River. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 2

GUWAHATI - SHILLONG

After breakfast we will drive to Shillong. En-route we will visit Barapani Umiam Lake, a majestic placid lake surrounded by sylvan hills. Upon arrival, check into a hotel. Later sightseeing of Shillong, visit Cathedral Church, Lady Hydari Park, the Orchidarium. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 3

SHILLONG – CHERRAPUNJEE

Early in the morning after breakfast, we will drive to Cherrapunjee, which is barely 65 km from Shillong. It is the wettest destination in the world and on your drive to Cherrapunjee, you will come across roaring waterfalls diving into unfathomable gorges. Enroute visit Elephant Falls & Shillong Peak. Upon arrival, check into a hotel. Later, we will visit the famous Nohsngithiang Falls, Nohkalikai Falls and Mawsmai Cave. Return to the hotel by evening for an overnight stay.

Day 4

CHERRAPUNJEE (TRIP TO LIVING ROOTS BRIDGE- DOUBLE DECKER)

Early in the morning after breakfast, we will go on a guided trek to visit the Double Decker living Routes Bridge, the most brilliant of all the living bridges found in the world. The trek from the resort will take about 05 hours. The scenery of the places is beyond description. Your will get amazed by walking over the roots that can appear quite scary in the beginning. However, the picturesque forest all around and the pleasant sound of waterfalls will compensate that scary feeling with unmatched glee. Return to the resort by evening for enjoying a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 5

CHERRAPUNJEE – MAWLYNNONG – DAWKI – SHILLONG

After breakfast drive to Mawlynnong – Asia’s cleanest village, Tree House, Living Root Bridge, Sky View Point, Balancing rock. After lunch proceed to Dawki, Umngot river. Evening back to Shillong and Overnight stay at hotel in Shillong..

Day 6

SHILLONG – NAMERI

In the morning we will proceed to the Nameri National Park, situated within the foothills of eastern Himalayas. Upon reaching, transfer to a nature camp. In the evening you are free to visit the nearby Pygmy Hog Foundation and learn about the species. The Pygmy Hogs are one of the world’s most endangered mammals. Overnight stay in the camp.

Day 7

SHILLONG– GUWAHATI DEPT

After breakfast we will drive to Guwahati airport or railway station for onward connection with cherishing memories of the tour.
