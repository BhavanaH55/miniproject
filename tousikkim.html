BEAUTY OF SIKKIM
Day 1

BAGDOGRA (IXB) AIRPORT to GANGTOK (5500 FT / 120 KMS / 5 HRS)

Upon your arrival at NJP (New Jalpaiguri) Railway Station, or Bagdogra Airport (if you are arriving by flight), you will be received by a representative of Tour My India. This is followed by an assisted transfer to a private vehicle. After arriving at the pre booked hotel, complete your check in formalities.

Day 2

GANGTOK - TSOMGO LAKE EXCURSION (12400 FT / 43 KMS / 3 HRS)

After breakfast, set off for a full day excursion to Lake Tsomgo. The lake has an average depth of 50 feet. The beauty of the lake will leave you in a trance like state. Spend some romantic moments here. Continue the drive to Baba Mandir, a holy site. Return to Gangtok. You also have the option of visiting Nathula Pass, situated on the India China border. However, this is subject to the condition that you obtain a permit. Nathula Pass remains closed on Monday and Tuesday.

Day 3

GANGTOK to DARJEELING (6700 FT / 102 KMS / 4 HRS)

Morning visit fort of Golconda is the former capital of the Qutub Shahi dynasty. Afternoon visit of the city. O/N at hotel.

Day 4

DARJEELING - SUNRISE & CITY TOUR

The day begins early at 3:45 AM. Itâ€™s dark when you arise! Drive to Tiger Hill to enjoy the spectacular sunrise over Khangchendzonga Peak. On the way back, visit Ghoom Monastery, Peace Memorial and Batista Loop. Return to the hotel for breakfast and freshening up. Post breakfast, set off for a sightseeing tour of the famous tourist attractions in Darjeeling. This includes Padmaja Naidu Zoological Park, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, Ropeway, Tensing Gumpo Rock, Tibetan Refugee Self Help Center, Tea Estate, Natural History Museum and Peace Pagoda. Return to the hotel. You can enjoy a leisurely stroll at the Mall Road in the evening. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 5

DARJEELING - BAGDOGRA (IXB) AIRPORT (105 KMS / 3-4 HRS)

Start your day with a hearty breakfast. That is followed by an assisted transfer to Bagdogra for your onward journey. This marks the end of the trip.
